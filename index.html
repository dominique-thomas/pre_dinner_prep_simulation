About

The purpose of this simulation is to provide learners with a fun and engaging experience while preparing for a dinner party. The simulation uses situated learning principle to create a relatable and immersive environment.

Learners can interact with a to-do list and an instruction manual, as well as receive responses from their character. Additionally, learners can explore different areas of the house and complete tasks in any order, offering a more authentic experience.

The concept and simulation were designed and developed by Dominique Thomas.

The last update for this simulation was made on 07/24/2024.
